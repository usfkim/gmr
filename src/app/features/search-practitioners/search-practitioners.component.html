Understanding License Status

Active

Active License

The practitioner's license is current and valid. They are authorized to practice within their scope.

Provisional

Provisional License

The practitioner has a temporary license, typically issued to new graduates or those completing requirements.

Suspended

Suspended License

The practitioner's license has been temporarily suspended. They are not authorized to practice.

Expired

Expired License

The practitioner's license has expired and needs renewal. They are not authorized to practice.

Verification Process

The Uganda Medical Registry verifies practitioners through a rigorous process:

  • 1

    Credential verification with issuing educational institutions

  • 2

    Confirmation of licensing with appropriate regulatory bodies

  • 3

    Background checks for disciplinary actions or malpractice

  • 4

    Regular updates to ensure current information

If you have concerns about a practitioner's credentials or license status, please contact us immediately.
