Frequently Asked Questions

What are the office hours for in-person visits?

Our main office is open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. The Verification Department is also open on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. All offices are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

How long does it take to receive a response to an email inquiry?

We strive to respond to all email inquiries within 24-48 business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our support helpline at +233 30 290 1235.

Can I schedule an appointment for licensing or verification services?

Yes, appointments are recommended for licensing and verification services to minimize wait times. You can schedule an appointment by calling our office or using the online appointment booking system on our website.

How can I check the status of my license application?

You can check the status of your license application by logging into your account on our website, calling our support helpline, or sending an email to {{'support@ugandamedicalregistry.com'}} with your application reference number.
