About Uganda Medical Registry

The official national platform for verification of licensed healthcare practitioners in Uganda

Our Vision & Mission

Guiding principles that drive our commitment to Uganda's healthcare system

Our Vision

To become Africa's most trusted, comprehensive, and digitally empowered medical registry — safeguarding public health and restoring confidence in healthcare through verified professionals and transparent data.

Our Mission

To digitally verify and connect every licensed medical, alternative, and allied health professional in Uganda, creating a centralized, government-supported platform that reduces fraud, protects lives, and promotes healthcare excellence.

Strategic goals that guide our operations and development

Regulatory Framework

The legal and regulatory foundation that guides our operations

Legal Mandate

  • Health Professions Regulatory Bodies Act

    Act 857 (2013) provides the legal framework for the regulation of healthcare professionals in Uganda.

  • Traditional Medicine Practice Act

    Act 575 (2000) establishes the regulatory framework for traditional and alternative medicine practitioners.

  • Data Protection Act

    Act 843 (2012) governs the collection, use, and protection of personal data, ensuring privacy and security.

  • Ministry of Health Directives

    Executive directives establishing UMR as the official verification platform for healthcare practitioners.

Compliance Standards

  • Data Security & Privacy

    Advanced encryption and security protocols protecting sensitive practitioner and user data.

  • Regular Auditing

    Quarterly system audits ensuring data accuracy, integrity, and compliance with regulatory requirements.

  • International Standards

    Alignment with WHO guidelines on healthcare workforce information systems and verification.

  • Professional Ethics

    Adherence to professional codes of conduct and ethical standards in healthcare verification.

Our Contribution to Uganda's Healthcare

How the Uganda Medical Registry strengthens the nation's healthcare ecosystem

Enhancing Public Trust

By providing transparent access to practitioner credentials, UMR builds public confidence in Uganda's healthcare system. Citizens can verify their healthcare providers' qualifications, ensuring they receive care from legitimate professionals.

Supporting Regulatory Bodies

UMR provides regulatory councils with a unified platform for monitoring compliance, managing renewals, and identifying unlicensed practitioners, strengthening the overall regulatory framework.

Empowering Healthcare Institutions

Hospitals, clinics, and healthcare facilities use UMR to verify staff credentials, streamline recruitment, and ensure compliance with licensing requirements, improving institutional quality and safety.

Advancing Uganda's Global Healthcare Position

By implementing international best practices in healthcare regulation, UMR enhances Uganda's standing in the global healthcare community and facilitates international mobility for Ugandan healthcare professionals.

Healthcare Workforce Distribution

UMR data provides valuable insights into healthcare workforce distribution across Uganda, helping identify gaps and inform policy decisions for equitable healthcare access.
