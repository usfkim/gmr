Who We Cover

Uganda Medical Registry covers licensed professionals under the following councils and regulatory bodies

Medical & Dental Council (MDC)

Regulating medical doctors, dentists, physician assistants, and related professionals

Traditional Medicine Practice Council (TMPC)

Overseeing traditional and alternative medicine practitioners across Uganda

Allied Health Professions Council (AHPC)

Regulating various allied health professionals providing essential healthcare services

Uganda Health Service (UHS)

National healthcare delivery and administration across public health facilities

Why UMR Matters

The Uganda Medical Registry provides essential services that benefit healthcare professionals, employers, and the general public

Public Confidence & Safety

Ensures that Ugandans can trust the qualifications of healthcare providers, promoting safety and confidence in the healthcare system.

Easy and Fast Verification

Provides instant access to practitioner credentials, eliminating lengthy verification processes and reducing administrative burden.

Reduces Fraudulent Practice

Helps identify and prevent unlicensed individuals from practicing medicine, protecting patients from potential harm.

Supports Regulation

Strengthens regulatory oversight by providing a centralized database for monitoring compliance with licensing requirements.

Centralized Access

Offers a single point of access for employers, regulators, and the public to verify practitioner credentials across multiple disciplines.

International Recognition

Facilitates international mobility for Ugandan healthcare professionals by providing verifiable credentials to foreign institutions.
